Why claymorphism

What makes the look

A quick primer on the signature traits of clay-inspired interfaces.

Gradient skins

Soft, pastel gradients replace flat fills to mimic hand-molded material and create depth without harsh shadows.

Long, blurred shadows

Ambient, offset shadows give every element a hovering feel, as if it has been lifted gently from the background.

Inner highlights

Subtle inset glows and top-edge highlights emulate the way light diffuses across soft, malleable materials.
